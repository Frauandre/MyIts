Beautiful places in the world

Very often we humans forget how beautiful our planet is and that’s why we tend not to respect it. After you see these places you will surely agree with me, let’s discover them together.

  1. Niagara falls

    2. The historical roots of Niagara Falls lie in the glaciation of Wisconsin finished about 10,000 years ago. The Great Lakes of America of the north and the Niagara River are the effect of the dissolution of this huge mass of ice. The glacier has moved in this area as a giant bulldozer moving rocks and earth and transforming the rivers in lakes, creating barriers with its debris, which in turn They created new canals that then became rivers.

  2. Ancient Chinese Wall

    3. Before the use of bricks, the Great Wall had been built primarily using rammed earth, stones and wood. During the Ming Dynasty, however, bricks were used in many areas of the wall, as were materials such as tile, lime, and stone. The size and weight of the bricks made them easier to work with than earth and stone, speeding up construction. In addition, bricks could withstand more weight and last longer than clay.

  3. Great Barrier Reef

    4. It is found off the coast of Queensland in northeastern Australia. The Great Barrier Reef can be seen from space and is the most large structure made of living organisms. The structure consists of billions of tiny organisms, known as coral polyps. The reef has a great biodiversity, and has been included as a Heritage of Humanity in 1981. CNN included it in its Seven Wonders of the World.

  4. Chronomaly Of Stonehenge

    5. Stonehenge is a Neolithic site located near Amesbury in Wiltshire, England, approximately 13 kilometers northwest of Salisbury.
